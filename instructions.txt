PREREQ: 
1) <username> must have sudo privs on the destination server, not requiring a password.
   Edit /etc/sudoers and add:
      <username>     ALL=(ALL)      NOPASSWD: ALL
2) ssh <username>@<server> and accept the server to your known_hosts
